Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Federal Prime Integrator?

We act as the lead contractor, managing all administrative and compliance burdens so you can focus on technical delivery.

How does Kurozato LLC differ from a consultant?

We don't just give advice; we take the lead on the contract and handle the actual reporting, FAR management, and audit-ready documentation.

Why do I need $1M+ in revenue to partner?

This ensures you have the operational capacity to perform on federal contracts while we handle the integration.

Do I need prior government experience?

No. We provide the expertise you lack to bridge the gap into the federal market.

What is FAR compliance?

The Federal Acquisition Regulation is the complex set of rules governing government purchases. We manage this entirely for you.

Will I lose control over my technical delivery?

Not at all. You focus on the work; we focus on the bureaucracy and administrative excellence.

What exactly is "audit-ready documentation"?

It's the standard of record-keeping required to pass government reviews. We build and maintain this for every contract.

Can you help if I don't have a CAGE or UEI?

Yes, we can guide you through the initial registration as part of our onboarding.

What industries do you serve?

We work with established commercial firms across all professional, technical, and administrative services.

How do you handle DCAA requirements?

We ensure all accounting and reporting meet Defense Contract Audit Agency standards.

What is the fee structure?

We operate on a prime integrator model, typically structured as a percentage of the contract value or a fixed management fee depending on the scope.

How do we communicate on a day-to-day basis?

You have a dedicated integrator lead who handles all government-facing communication and compliance reporting.

Can you help with security clearances?

We can assist in navigating the process for facility and personnel clearances as required by specific contracts.

How long is the onboarding process?

Typically 2-4 weeks to align systems and identify target opportunities.

Do you assist with proposal development?

Yes, we lead the administrative and compliance portions of the proposal while you provide the technical input.

What happens if our contract is audited?

Kurozato manages the audit process directly, providing all necessary documentation and responding to government inquiries.

Do you provide the workforce?

You provide the technical experts and delivery team; we provide the federal administrative infrastructure.

How do I know if my firm is a good fit?

If you have a proven commercial track record but find federal regulations overwhelming, we are your ideal partner.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Kurozato LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the information provided to us by our clients, partners, and website visitors.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our website and services, including:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, telephone number, job title, and professional contact details
  • Business and Vendor Qualification Information: Company name, address, NAICS codes, UEI, CAGE/NCAGE codes, SAM registration details
  • Financial and Commercial Information: Business revenue, financial capability indicators, banking/payment details
  • Compliance and Due Diligence Information: Debarment/suspension status, audit-ready documentation
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, usage patterns collected through cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Facilitate federal contract participation and manage FAR compliance
  • Vetting and Qualification: Evaluate capacity, eligibility, and compliance posture
  • Communication: Provide updates on contract opportunities and regulatory changes
  • Compliance and Legal Obligations: Maintain audit-ready documentation and fulfill reporting requirements

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. We may disclose your information to:

  • Federal government agencies for contract performance and compliance
  • Service providers (including Netlify) who help us operate our website
  • Legal authorities when required by law
